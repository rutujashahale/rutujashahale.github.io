Project:- "Online Retail Sales Analysis" || Power Bi

• Insights of the Project:- 

•  Monthly Revenue Trends:
 - Revenue shows significant growth starting in August, peaking in November at $1.5M, and then dropping sharply in December.
 - Consistent revenue around $0.5M to $0.76M from January to July.

•  Country-Wise Performance:
 - The Netherlands, EIRE, and Germany have the highest quantities but not necessarily the highest revenues.
 - France and Australia show a balance between quantity and revenue, with France having a notably high revenue despite a smaller quantity compared to some other countries.
 - Smaller markets like Belgium and Norway contribute relatively lower quantities and revenues.

•  Top Customers:
- CustomerID 14646 is the highest revenue generator with $0.27M.
 - A steep drop in revenue after the top three customers, indicating a potential focus area for customer retention and growth.
